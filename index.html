About

DOIDOI is a Seoul-based media artist exploring unusual sensations in everyday life. She focuses on defining the blurry line between the virtual and real worlds. By transforming sensory elements into interactive content, she expands how we experience our senses in virtual environments.

With a background in visual communication design and interaction development, she operates DOIDOI Studio, blending technology and art to evoke emotional impact and pursue a deeper understanding of human nature.

Info

DOIDOI explores phenomenology, cognitive science, molecular gastronomy, and gastrophysics to expand her understanding of the world.


She specializes in interactive projects with Unreal Engine and TouchDesigner, sharing her research on her blog↗. Her past work includes motion-based technologies like Kinect, LeapMotion, and MediaPipe, and more recently, interactive touchscreen projects with LiDAR sensors.
