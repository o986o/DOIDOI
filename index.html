about

DOIDOI is a Seoul-based media artist exploring unusual sensations in everyday life. DOIDOI focuses on defining the blurry line between the virtual and real worlds. Through the work, DOIDOI aims to expand the way we experience our senses.

With a background in visual communication design and interaction development, DOIDOI operates DOIDOI Studio, blending technology and art to evoke emotional impact and pursue a deeper understanding of human nature.
