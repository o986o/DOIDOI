scan 2 taste

Installation

2025, Web-based Interactive Video Art
in Gandong Art Center, Seoul
from August 14, 2025 to September 4, 2025

“Scan 2 Taste” reframes the QR code not as a digital utility, but as a threshold to simulated sensation.

《Scan 2 Taste》는 QR 코드를 단순한 디지털 도구가 아닌, 시뮬레이션된 감각의 입구로 재구성한다.
